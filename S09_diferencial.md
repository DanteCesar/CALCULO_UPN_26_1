# Ejercicios de Derivadas de Funciones Trigonométricas

## 📘 Deriva las siguientes funciones:

1. $$f(x)=\sin(x)$$  
2. $$f(x)=\cos(x)$$  
3. $$f(x)=\tan(x)$$  
4. $$f(x)=\cot(x)$$  
5. $$f(x)=\sec(x)$$  
6. $$f(x)=\csc(x)$$  

---

## 📗 Aplica reglas básicas (constante, suma, producto simple):

7. $$f(x)=3\sin(x)$$  
8. $$f(x)=5\cos(x)-2\sin(x)$$  
9. $$f(x)=x\sin(x)$$  
10. $$f(x)=x^2\cos(x)$$  
11. $$f(x)=\sin(x)+\cos(x)$$  
12. $$f(x)=\tan(x)+x$$  

---

## 📙Deriva:

13. $$f(x)=\sin(3x)$$  
14. $$f(x)=\cos(2x+1)$$  
15. $$f(x)=\tan(5x)$$  
16. $$f(x)=\sin(x^2)$$  
17. $$f(x)=\cos(4x^3)$$  

---

## 📕 Usa regla del producto, cociente y cadena:

18. $$f(x)=x^2\sin(3x)$$  
19. $$f(x)=\frac{\sin(x)}{x}$$  
20. $$f(x)=\frac{x\cos(x)}{\sin(x)}$$  
21. $$f(x)=\sin(x)\cos(x)$$  
22. $$f(x)=\tan(x^2+1)$$  

---

## 📒 Reto

23. $$f(x)=\frac{\sin(2x)}{1+x^2}$$  
24. $$f(x)=x^3\tan(4x)$$  
25. $$f(x)=\sec(x)\tan(x)$$  
26. $$f(x)=\ln(\sin(x))$$  
27. $$f(x)=e^{\cos(x)}$$
